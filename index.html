About the Event

Ideat-a-thon 2025 is a 24-hour innovation sprint hosted at Chandigarh University, organized by Tamboo Baba. Compete in real-world challenges across 5 innovation domains!

  • 5 Diverse Problem Tracks
  • Mentorship and Collaboration
  • Exciting Prizes and Recognition

Guidelines

  • Team of 1–3 members
  • One track per team only
  • Submit via official PPT template (Download)
  • No extra slides allowed
  • Original ideas only – plagiarism leads to disqualification
  • On-ground presence at CU is mandatory

Choose Your Track

  1. Technology – AI, Automation, Digital Access
  2. Healthcare – Mental Health, Diagnostics
  3. Environment – Sustainability & Waste
  4. Education – Digital & Inclusive Learning
  5. Entrepreneurship – Startups & MSMEs
